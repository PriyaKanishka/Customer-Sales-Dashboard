# ☁️ Customer Sales Dashboard – Tableau

## 🔍 Project Overview
This Customer Sales Dashboard tracks online business performance and 
demonstrates the value of marketing efforts. It provides a clear view 
of the top 10 customers, their month-wise sales trends, and sub-category 
wise sales breakdown to support data-driven business decisions.

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Customer names and sales data
- Monthly sales figures
- Sub-category wise product sales
- Time series data from 2019 to 2022

## 🔑 KPIs Tracked
- Top 10 customers by total sales
- Month-wise sales per customer
- Sub-category wise sales performance
- Sales trends over time

## 📈 Key Insights
- Chairs and Phones are the highest revenue generating sub-categories 
  at 335,768 and 331,843 respectively
- Sean Miller and Tamara Chand are among the highest value customers
- Sales peaked in December 2022 at 118,455 indicating strong 
  year-end performance
- Binders and Machines also show significant sales contribution 
  at 207,355 and 189,925

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus retention efforts on top 10 high value customers to 
  protect revenue
- Prioritise inventory and marketing for Chairs and Phones 
  given their dominant sales contribution
- Leverage year-end sales patterns to plan targeted 
  promotional campaigns

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Tableau
- Excel / CSV

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Word Cloud showing Top 10 Customers by sales volume
- Month-wise sales trend line chart from 2019 to 2022
- Sub-category wise sales bar chart
- Interactive customer selection to filter all charts dynamically

This dashboard gives business owners and marketing teams a clear picture
